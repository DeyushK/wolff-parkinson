Wolff Parkinson White Syndrome

Symptoms

If you have WPW syndrome, you may experience episodes where your heart suddenly starts racing,
before stopping or slowing down abruptly.This rapid heart rate is called supraventricular tachycardia (SVT).

During an episode, you may experience symptoms such as:

-a pounding or fluttering heartbeat (heart palpitations)

-feeling lightheaded or dizzy

-shortness of breath

-chest pain

-passing out (fainting)

These episodes can last for seconds, minutes or hours.
